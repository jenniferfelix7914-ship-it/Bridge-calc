What was added for a resume/demo-safe build
The workflow is designed around the appointment-based / medication synchronization model: align chronic refills to one pickup date, review the profile before the pickup, and reduce unnecessary trips while supporting adherence.
  • PHI guardrails: no name, DOB, address, phone, doctor, Rx number, or free-text patient notes in the live app.
  • Print Plan: produces a clean paper plan with blank patient identifiers, target date, bridge quantities, risks, and a call checklist.
  • Med List: can print either the sync-planning meds or a blank general medication list, with patient-information fields completed only on paper.
  • Workflow flags: actual on-hand count override, insurance-pay restriction, high-cost estimate toggle, early-fill risk, and vacation quantity check.
  • No persistence: no localStorage, cookies, or database. Refreshing clears the session.
